Audio Aspects of my Escape Room

The audio style I'm going to use for my game is not much but the way I'm going to use them will help create the style of how the game is indented to feel, for example having a typing sound for when you press a button. Also, there will be some narrative sounds like the typing sound but not the same as receiving a message to you from someone else. As well as the background audio I'm going to be using will add to the feeling of being trapped and under pressure by hearing whisper's distant screams, this will add to the feel of my game.
